Q: How does QR check-in and badge printing work?
A: When someone registers, they get a unique QR code. At the event, staff scan it with the check-in app — badge prints on demand in seconds. Color-coded by role. Handles walk-ins and group check-in too. No pre-printed badges, no lines, no chaos.

A: Multiple ticket types — VIP, general, sponsor, speaker — each with different access levels. Custom registration forms with smart logic. Group tickets, coupon codes, upselling for add-ons like parking or hotel. Three fee models so the organizer chooses who absorbs transaction costs.

Q: How does the mobile app work?
A: Attendees get personalized schedules, real-time push notifications, AI matchmaking to connect with relevant people, digital business card exchange, and live session access — all from their phone.

Q: How do sponsor dashboards work?
